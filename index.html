Myers Family Reunion 2014

re·union noun \(ˌ)rē-ˈyün-yən\: an organized gathering of people who have not been together for a long time

In the spring of 2013, John, Emily, Lilly and Maggie Myers came to Buffalo for a weekend and were nice enough to include Deanna and me in their plans for an evening out. It was great to catch up and we all talked about how we don't see each other enough. We decided that a Myers reunion in Buffalo was long overdue. It's time!.

We formed a committee of "cousins" and have started the process of planning the reunion. In the next several months leading up to the reunion we will use email and this web site to keep people informed so bookmark the site and visit often. Let's get this party started! --Sean

Date: August 8-9, 2014
Where: Buffalo NY

FAQ

How may days should I plan for?
Most people will be arriving on Friday the 8th but some will be arriving on the 7th.
Where is everyone staying?
Most people are staying at the Embassy Suites in downtown Buffalo but there are plenty of hotels in the area if you prefer to stay somewhere else. We are trying to keep everyone in the same area if we can.
What kind of activities are planned?
We have events planned for Friday and Saturday evening and will compile a list of the many things that can be done in the Buffalo area while you are here including fun things for all ages.

Anyone is welcome to provide their ideas or suggestions. We have a small committee handling the logistics but we are just now starting to put the details together. Let Libby know if you want to participate.

Things to Do in Buffalo

Buffalo has lots to do in the summertime. Here is a sampling.

The Waterfront

There is plenty to do around the water in Buffalo. Here is a list of some of the things to checkout:

  • Canalside - great activities including Thursday night concerts, boat tours, and restaurants
  • Wilkeson Pointe Park - great views of the lake, slides, and other things for kids

Culture and History

What would Buffalo be without its history.

  • Frank Lloyd Wright's Darwin Martin House - a must see
  • Albright-Knox Art Gallery - the new director has made some interesting changes

Restaurants and Nightlife

There are plenty of restaurants in Buffalon area to satisfy everyone and the the list grows monthly. Here are some of our favorites:

  • The Tap Room at the Lafayette Hotel - one of the great restoration projects in Buffalo
  • Betty's - great for breakfast and lunch.
  • The Liberty Hound - right on the waterfront at Canalside
  • Dinosaur Bar-B-Que - justy opened and less than a block from the Avant Hotel
  • Gabriel's Gate - my favorite place for wings

Other Outdoor Activities

  • Frank Lloyd Wright's Darwin Martin House - a must see
  • Albright-Knox Art Gallery - the new director has made some interesting changes

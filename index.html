Myers Family Reunion 2014

re·union noun \(ˌ)rē-ˈyün-yən\ : an organized gathering of people who have not been together for a long time


In the spring of 2013, John, Emily, Lilly and Maggie Myers came to Buffalo for a weekend and were nice enough to include Deanna and me in their plans for an evening out. It was great to catch up and we all talked about how we don't see each other enough. We decided that a Myers reunion in Buffalo was long overdue. It's time! So, the next generations of Myers's picked up the baton and formed a reunion committee. With help from Libby, Tommy R., Sheila Marie, John R., Julia, Emily, Lily, Uncle Mike, Uncle John, and others, we've made some progress.
In the next several months leading up to the reunion we will use email and this web site to keep people informed so bookmark the site and visit often. Let's get this party started!
--Sean

FAQ

Do I need to license any additional software like ArcGIS to run Vetro?
No, Vetro is an entirely secure, cloud-based application that is built using open source tools and does not require any 3rd party licensing.
Where is my data being stored?
All data is stored securely on an Amazon Elastic Computing (EC2) instance.
How will I be charged for using Vetro?
You will pay for Vetro through a monthly subscription. The price depends on the size of your network - not the number of users.
What does Vetro mean?
Vetro is the Italian word for glass, the material that makes up a fiber strand.

Vetro

A Fiber Network Planning and Management Tool


How will Vetro help me expand my fiber network business?

Vetro makes it easier to target your fiber network investment and manage it for the long term.

  • View your fiber network assets including cables, poles, material, and equipment.
  • Stimulate demand for your fiber service by engaging neighborhoods with incentives and status updates.
  • Quickly assess the costs and benefits of network expansion projects.

Vetro has three main features:

  1. Asset inventory mapping and display - planners and engineers can view network assets, lit buildings, data centers, potential customers, rights-of-way, conduits, and other features through a simple mapping interface.
  2. Demand generation and tracking - sales people and planners can estimate and track the demand for their fiber services in particular neighborhoods and regions.
  3. Project Design and Planning - fiber planners and engineers can easily design and plan new projects including estimating the type and quantity of equipment that is required, poles and/or conduits that can be used to place the fiber, where splice points will occur, and what points of presence (POPs) will be used.

FAQ

Do I need to license any additional software like ArcGIS to run Vetro?
No, Vetro is an entirely secure, cloud-based application that is built using open source tools and does not require any 3rd party licensing.
Where is my data being stored?
All data is stored securely on an Amazon Elastic Computing (EC2) instance.
How will I be charged for using Vetro?
You will pay for Vetro through a monthly subscription. The price depends on the size of your network - not the number of users.
What does Vetro mean?
Vetro is the Italian word for glass, the material that makes up a fiber strand.
